This file contains interesting datasets which can be used as a basis for missions. This list is not exhaustive, feel free to add more datasets if you find something interesting. 

# General
- [UCI Machine Learning Repository](https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/index.php): A collection of databases, domain theories, and data generators widely used by the machine learning community for empirical analysis of machine learning algorithms.
- [Kaggle Datasets](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets): A platform for data science competitions that also hosts a large collection of datasets across various domains.
- [Google Dataset Search](https://datasetsearch.research.google.com/): A search engine for datasets across the web, allowing you to find datasets stored in thousands of repositories across the web.
- [Data.gov](https://www.data.gov/): The home of the U.S. Government’s open data, providing access to a wide range of datasets from various government agencies.
- [World Bank Open Data](https://data.worldbank.org/): A collection of datasets related to global development, including economic, social, and environmental data.
- [Quandl](https://www.quandl.com/): A platform for financial, economic, and alternative data, offering access to millions of datasets from various sources.

# Stock data
- [ETF] (https://aktienfinder.net/etf-finder): A comprehensive database of exchange-traded funds (ETFs) with detailed information on their holdings, performance, and other relevant metrics.
- [Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/): A popular platform for financial news, data, and analysis, providing access to stock prices, historical data, and other financial information.
- [Alpha Vantage](https://www.alphavantage.co/): A provider of free APIs for real-time and historical stock market data, including stock prices, technical indicators, and more
- [IEX Cloud](https://iexcloud.io/): A financial data platform that provides access to real-time and historical stock market data, including stock prices, news, and other financial information

# Supply Chain data
- [AI Supply Chain Data](https://aisupplychain.vercel.app/): A collection of datasets related to supply chain management, including data on logistics, inventory management, and demand forecasting.

# TU Dortmund Datasets
 [TU Dortmund Datasets](https://www.tu-dortmund.de/tu-dortmund/de/forschung/forschungsdaten/datenbank/index.html): A collection of datasets from various research projects conducted at TU Dortmund University, covering a wide range of topics and disciplines.
Furthermore we have local licences for other datasets which we can use for the missions. If you are interested in using any of these datasets, please contact us and we will provide you with access.
